PlayGround

Our school has two playgrounds, and two soccer fields. We have one playground at top level and one at bottom level, and the grades are seperated from grade 1-4 and grade 4-7. Each week the grades switch from the level they are currently on the other level. Grade 4's get to pick which level they want to go on. Each level has two soccer nets, and a playground.
